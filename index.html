PHONE CONS.

smartphone is a mobile-enabled communication device used for voice and text communications, which also allows access to the Internet and runs multiple applications. The phone has many downsides, most notably technology addiction: over-reliance on the phone can lead to technology addiction, which negatively affects social interactions. Effect on face-to-face communication: Overuse of the phone can reduce the chances of face-to-face and personal communication. Attention Distraction: Using the phone in the car while driving leads to distraction, which increases the risk of a traffic accident. In addition to a lack of privacy: sharing a lot of personal information across apps and social media leads to a loss of privacy. Spending a lot of time on the phone increases the risk of myopic as it affects the eyes in several ways: Eye strain: Constant focus on the small screen for long periods can cause eyes strain and lead to fatigue and a feeling of dryness. Screen light The blue light emitted by the phone screen affects sleep and increases the chance of sleep problems. Visibility distortion: Focusing on the screen for a long time at close range may lead to short-sightedness. Regular eye rest should be taken and phone use minimized to minimize the effects of these issues. In addition, the phone causes increased stress, distraction and tension due to smart devices.
